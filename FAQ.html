Frequently Asked Questions

Our museum is open from Tuesday to Sunday, 10 AM to 6 PM. We are closed on Mondays.
Tickets can be booked online through our website or purchased at the museum's ticket counter. Please check the "Events" section for more details on available exhibitions.
Photography is allowed in certain areas of the museum. However, the use of flash and tripods is prohibited. Please follow the signage and guidelines provided at the entrance.
To submit artwork for exhibition, please visit our "Artist Submission" page and follow the instructions provided. We accept submissions from both emerging and established artists. Ensure that your work adheres to our submission guidelines.
